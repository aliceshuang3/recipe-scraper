About Us


Hi there, we're Jacquelyn Chow and Alice Huang!
We're Swarthmore College students studying Computer Science, and we created Novice Chef for fun during the summer of 2020. We were inspired to create this app because we are also beginner cooks and always have a hard time using up ingredients in our fridge.

Hopefully, with our site in your arsenal, deciding on what to make and how to use up old ingredients is much easier.

How To Use:
Click on the Search tab to get started with your search for specific ingredients you want to use up or try out our random recipe generator if you want to cook something new. You can also save recipes to come back to later if you create an account!

Current Sites In Our Database:
Gimme Some Oven, Pinch Of Yum, Damn Delicious,
Tasty, Bon Appetit, Minimalist Baker

We'd love to know how we can make our site better, so please let us know about more features or recipe sites you want to see in our database!
